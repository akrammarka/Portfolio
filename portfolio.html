Shipping Label Scanner

Challenge: Manually identifying shipping carriers and extracting tracking numbers from labels is time-consuming and error-prone, especially when processing multiple packages.

Solution: Developed an intelligent computer vision system that uses camera input to automatically detect and identify shipping labels. The system recognizes major carriers (UPS, FedEx), extracts tracking numbers using OCR technology, and parses shipping details in real-time, streamlining package processing workflows.
